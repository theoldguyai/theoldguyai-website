The Story

One Developer.
Three Brands.

Overhill Valley Digital Ventures started as a question: what happens when you give one experienced person the full arsenal of modern AI tools and tell them to build a media company from scratch?

The answer is this. Three newsletters, built from the ground up, running on AI pipelines — publishing consistently, growing organically, and proving every day that the future of media is solo, lean, and brutally effective.

The Old Guy AI exists because solopreneurs deserve sharp, honest AI coverage. AI for Every1 exists because clarity is a public good. Senior Intelligence AI exists because the generation that built the modern world deserves to lead the next one too.
